Career History

USAF

Growing up I had two career aspirations - computers and aviation maintenance. After High School there were no online courses available and my working-class family did not have the money to send me to college. I enlisted in the United States Air Force and signed up to be a jet engine technician. I made it a career and served for 20 years, retiring in 2019. During that time I was challenged with technical training and leadership courses. I advanced through the enlisted ranks where I eventually retired at E-7. Half-way through my military career I found Capella University, which allowed me to complete my computing degree completely online, which I needed as I could be moved around the world at any moments notice. I continued to take advantage of my education benefits and will be completing my Masters degree in 2021. The Air Force sent me to many places - I spent 7 years in Japan, 3 years in California, and the rest in my home state of Texas, with a temporary duty locations and a deployment to Iraq in between.

Duty Locations

  1. Lackland AFB Texas, Bootcamp, 1999
  2. Sheppard AFB Texas, Technical Training, 2000
  3. Misawa AB Japan, Jet Engine Journeyman, 2000-2003
  4. Edwards AFB California, F-22 Evaluator, 2003-2005
  5. Misawa AB Japan, Jet Engine Craftsman, 2005-2009
  6. Sheppard AFB Texas, Jet Engine Instructor, 2009-2013
  7. Dyess AFB Texas, Jet Engine Superintendent, 2013-2019

Significant Decorations

  • X1 Meritorious Serive Medal
  • X3 Air Force Commendation Medal
  • X2 Air Force Achievement Medal

Present

Now that I have retired and completed my education, I can now persue my second career aspiraion - computers. I was hired on by a water utilities company and was given the position of Applications Adminsitrator. I oversee applications that deal with water management and telemetry systems. I have written scripts to help with the management of data flow as well as creating custom reports for my customers. I am happy to have been given the opportunity to chase both career aspirations I had as a teen.
